Mario Kart DS

Unuploadable because:

This one should have been uploadable, but it was taking forever to the point where I decided to put it here instead. After that decision, I added the screenshot feature.

Mario Kart DS Controls and Things You Should Know:

W for gas, A/D to steer, SPACE to drift. ENTER to select on the selection screens. SHIFT to take screenshot. Resolution slider sets resolution of textured tri filler, setting to 0 will make things further out less detailed, but closer things will have high detail. Wireframe further out will be filled in as you get closer, it was a way to show all of the scene and still have performance. Note that it was made to run in a laggy environment, so the framerate limit is 15 FPS. Don't be surprised if it is running below 20 FPS.
